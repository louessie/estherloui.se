ESTHER KLINGBIEL // UNTITLED SERIES, 2014





Photography: Alex Karabel, Esther Klingbiel
Mixed media: Alex Karabel (left), Esther Klingbiel (right)


Using a variety of mixed media, the aim of this project was to explore differing views and interpretations of public life in New York City through creative interventions, with photographs as a mutual starting point. Using these pictures, which Alex and I took together, we were able to create our own unique aesthetic narratives.
