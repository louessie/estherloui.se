Dear Esther Klingbiel

Yes, yes, yes!!! Congratulations on your acceptance to Pratt Institute for the fall 2014 semester! I am delighted to welcome you to Pratt's talented incoming class. This year's selection process was extremely difficult with over 6500 freshman applications for 660 spots. Your official acceptance packet was mailed yesterday, March 24.

Submitting Your Deposit: your next step, if you intend to enroll at Pratt, is to deposit online by May 1 for immediate confirmation and for a record of submission.

Your Pratt ID is: 2390116.

Your major code is U2240F.

You will need this information when you deposit, so make a note of it or print this email. The deposits are due by May 1 to reserve your space. Make sure if you mail your deposit that it arrives by May 1. We strongly encourage you to submit online, however, to ensure immediate receipt.

To Deposit: In order to submit both the enrollment and the housing deposits online, you must submit one and then go back and submit the next using the link above. If you intend to live on campus, it is essential that you submit your housing deposit May. You will receive a housing selections email from Residential Life within a week or two from the time you submit the deposit after May 1.

Financial aid awards are enclosed with your mailed packet if you submitted the FAFSA ( US citizen or permanent residents only). You may contact the financial aid office at 718 636-3599 once you receive your package if you have questions.

Accepted Student Days: join us for our accepted student days on either Saturday, April 12, or Sunday, April 13, depending on your major. Please make sure you register for the correct date as specific majors are offered each day. Accepted Student Day includes visits and discussions bythe chair of each academic department and campus tours. Lunch will be provided. Accepted Student Days are from 9:30am-2pm. Register online.

April 12 offers the following majors only: architecture, art history, art and design education, digital arts (2D animation, 3D animation and motion arts, and interactive arts), construction management, fine arts, interior design, critical and visual studies, photography, writing, undecided/foundation, and two-year associate degree programs.
April 13 offers the following majors: communications design (graphic design, illustration, and advertising art direction), fashion design, film, and industrial design.

Make sure you check out Pratt's new website launched this morning! You'll find lots of new features in addition to the same layout of the old site. Check out our virtual tour, gallery of student work, and student life, including athletics, living and dining on campus, student activities, and more. .

On behalf of the faculty, administration, and students, I congratulate you on your acceptance. We look forward to welcoming you as a new member of the Pratt community!

Sincerely,

Judith Aaron
