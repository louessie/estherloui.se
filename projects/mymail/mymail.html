ESTHER KLINGBIEL // MYMA.IL, 2016





My first membership to the internet was the email address I made when I was 11—and it is the email address I still use. I wanted to create a space where I could see how I developed and matured from the age of 11 (in 2007) to the age of 20 (2016) solely through emails I sent.

Each year contains three emails I sent that load randomly (when you refresh the page, another randomly chosen email appears).
