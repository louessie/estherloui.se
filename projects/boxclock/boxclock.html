ESTHER KLINGBIEL // BOXCLO.CK, 2016





Based on visual synesthetic experiences triggered by periods of time, this site is a visual clock that represents the minute, hour, and week of any given date by using Javascript to call certain visual queues as well as looping CSS animations.


View project here.
Download Chrome extension here.


The time of day (dawn, midday, evening, night, etc.) is represented by the image on the left-hand face.

Seconds are represented by the blinking bar in on the left-hand face.

The day of the week is represented by the background color on the right-hand face. The color was decided by my synesthetic experience of time.

Minutes are represented by the bar on the right-hand face. As the minute draws closer to its 60 second mark, the bar gradually increases in opacity.

The month is represented by the background color on the bottom face. The color was decided by my synesthetic experience of time.

Hours are represented by the circle on the bottom face. As the hour draws closer to its 60 minute mark, the circle gradually increases in opacity.
