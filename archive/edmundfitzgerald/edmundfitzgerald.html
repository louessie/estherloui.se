ESTHER KLINGBIEL // THE WRECK OF THE EDMUND FITZGERALD, 2017





In 1975, the American freighter SS Edmund Fitzgerald sank in a storm on Lake Superior. The event was the inspiration for Gordon Lightfoot's 1976 song The Wreck of the Edmund Fitzgerald.

This project outlines a brief history of the event, as well as sources linked to it based on Lightfoot's lyrics.
