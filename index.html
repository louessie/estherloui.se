Hello! Thank you for visiting my site. I'm a graphic designer born and based in Brooklyn, New York.

In May 2019, I graduated from Parsons School of Design with a BFA in Communication Design. I also obtained a BA in Psychology from Eugene Lang College at The New School.

Currently I am working as a freelance graphic designer in web, print, and branding. I am also looking for full-time employment.

This website was launched in the fall of 2017 to showcase my work as well as to exercise my coding (all done from scratch!).

I am especially interested in typography and publication/editorial design. If you would like to contact me, shoot an email to esther.klingbiel@gmail.com, or check out more links on my "Contact" page.

Thanks for stopping by!
